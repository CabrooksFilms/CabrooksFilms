Be an Ambassador!

Do you have a passion for photography, use social media every day, and want to help the environment? Cabrooks Films Ambassadors program is currently looking for new Ambassadors ages 16-25 to promote our services and save the environment one step at a time. Ambassadors would be responsible for promoting services and lead possible clients to Cabrooks Films through social media platforms such as Facebook and Instagram as well as begin our “give back” to the environment by participating and/or starting local cleanups in your community. Though unpaid, for every action there is a point system that tracks each ambassador’s progress and for every 200 points, an incentive is rewarded by Cabrooks Films. Ambassadors also become a part of a virtual community that can help them interact with others that they might not otherwise meet. This opens doors for them in the future as far as combining talents for future endeavors as well as mentorship with high school and college kids. Fill out the application below and see if you have what it takes to be a Cabrooks Films Brand Ambassador!
