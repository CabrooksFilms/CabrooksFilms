Our Services

Headshots

Our headshots focus on capturing clients from the waist up. Usually popular with performing artists as well as business professionals, these photos capture our clients in an outdoor setting that best highlights their craft. This photoshoot typically lasts 30 minutes.

Lifestyle

Our lifestyle photoshoots focus on capturing you living your best life. Widely popular with aspiring models and dancers, these photos highlight a moment where you are looking and feeling your best regardless of the occasion. This photoshoot typically lasts one hour and comes with a one minute highlight reel of your shoot.

Maternity

Our maternity photoshoots focus on capturing soon to be moms with their significant others in the glow of the sun. Siblings and immediate family are welcome and encouraged to be a part of this special moment. This photoshoot typically lasts one hour and comes with a one minute highlight reel of your shoot.

Couples/Engagement

Our couple and engagement photoshoots focus on capturing couples young and old. Whether you just got engaged or have been married for 50 years, we want to highlight this moment in time and preserve it for years to come. This photoshoot typically lasts one hour and comes with a one minute highlight reel of your shoot.

Family/Group

Our family and group photoshoots focus on capturing families large and small. Whether you want the whole family or just you and your pup, we want to snap a picture of it all. Plus these photos are perfect for your next Christmas card! This photoshoot typically lasts one hour and comes with a one minute highlight reel of your shoot.

Birthday/Special Occasion

Our birthday and special occasion photoshoots focus on capturing your special day. This is a perfect shoot to capture a first birthday, 15th or 16th birthday, prom, and so much more. Though we do not provide props for these shoots, we work with you to figure out the perfect props for your shoots. This photoshoot typically lasts one hour and comes with a one minute highlight reel of your shoot.
