Locations

New York City

Cabrooks Films currently caters to clients in all five boroughs of New York City. Our photoshoots are typically shot in Manhattan and range between various locations across the island. With all the beautiful parks and scenic views of the city, we are confident that we can find the perfect location for your shoot.

New Jersey

Cabrooks Films currently caters to clients only in Hudson County. With a perfect view of the Manhattan skyline as well as various parks and artistic style, we are prepared to match you with the location of your dreams for your photoshoot.

South Florida

Coming November 2019, Cabrooks Films will officially be taking South Florida by storm. With its beautiful beaches, parks, and art deco charm, our team is excited to match you with a location that will make your photos a conversation starter.
