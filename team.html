About Us

Cabrooks Films is an outdoor photography company that specializes in capturing moments beyond the photograph.


There is a high interest in today’s society to document every minute of our lives and share it with others. Cabrooks Films photoshoots not only offer high quality digital photos that can be easily downloaded through their clients personalized photo gallery, but they also include a 1-minute memento video that highlights the best moments of a session that a photo just can’t capture.


Founded by Angelica Cabrera and Josh Brooks in 2016, they held nothing but one goal at its core: to tell a story. Cabrooks Films instills strong values of camaraderie in its staff and works diligently to maintain sense of unity among all teams in the three locations. They are a dedicated team of individuals who are focused to making every photoshoot unique and simply capture every moment.
